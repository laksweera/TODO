## Getting Started

To begin using the Task Management System, open the application in your web browser.

## Registration

1. **Open the Registration Page:**
   Navigate to the registration page by clicking on the "Sign Up" button if you are not already on the registration page.

2. **Enter Your Details:**
   Fill in the required fields:
   - **First Name:** Enter your first name.
   - **Last Name:** Enter your last name.
   - **Email:** Enter a valid email address.
   - **Password:** Enter a password that is at least 8 characters long.

3. **Submit the Form:**
   Click the "Sign Up" button to submit the registration form. If all details are correct, you will be registered successfully and redirected to the login page.

## Logging In

1. **Open the Login Page:**
   Navigate to the login page by clicking on the "Sign In" button.

2. **Enter Your Credentials:**
   Fill in your registered email and password.

3. **Submit the Form:**
   Click the "Sign In" button to log in. If your credentials are correct, you will be redirected to the dashboard.

## Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you will be taken to the dashboard, where you can view and manage your tasks.

- **Task List:** A list of your tasks will be displayed, showing the task name, description, and status.
- **Add New Task:** A button at the top right corner allows you to add new tasks.

## Managing Tasks

### Adding a New Task

1. **Open Add Task Modal:**
   Click the "Add new" button located at the top right corner of the dashboard.

2. **Enter Task Details:**
   Fill in the task details in the modal:
   - **Task Name:** Enter the name of the task.
   - **Description:** Enter a brief description of the task.
   - **Status:** Select the status of the task (In Progress or Complete).

3. **Save the Task:**
   Click the "Save" button to add the task to your list.

### Editing a Task

1. **Open Edit Task Modal:**
   Click the "Edit" button next to the task you want to edit.

2. **Update Task Details:**
   Modify the task details in the modal.

3. **Save Changes:**
   Click the "Save" button to update the task details.

### Deleting a Task

1. **Open Delete Task Modal:**
   Click the "Delete" button next to the task you want to delete.

2. **Confirm Deletion:**
   Confirm the deletion in the modal. The task will be removed from your list.

### Updating Task Status

1. **Change Status:**
   Select the new status (In Progress or Complete) from the dropdown next to the task.

2. **Confirm Update:**
   Confirm the status update when prompted. The task status will be updated.

## Error Handling

If there are any errors (e.g., invalid email, password too short), an error message will be displayed. Ensure that you provide the correct information as prompted by the error messages.

For further assistance, you can contact support or refer to the application's help section.

Happy task managing!
